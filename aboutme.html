Hobies and interests

Animals

As a child, I didn't have the opportunity to keep a pet at home, but that's why whenever I saw a kitten or a dog on the street, I went and petted them. I have only kept small fish at home, but unfortunately they died, because they don't live long. I also really like to ride horses, but I don't do it professionally, only sometimes as a hobby. I can say that animals are an important part of my life, as much as music is.

Music

I love to listen to slow music, but sometimes also pop. The genre of the song changes depending on how I feel at that moment. Most of the time I listen to Turkish and English songs and less in any other language. Sometimes I post a song on my social media that really describes my emotions at that moment.

Sport

If we talk about sports, I have a lot of experience. When I was in elementary school, I coached handball for 5 years. Then, at the end of primary school and the beginning of secondary school, I coached volleyball for three years. A few months ago I also went to kangoo jumping, but I quit because of university. Sports have always been a part of recreation and hobby and I want to be active in such things as long as I can.

Relax time

When I want to relax, besides listening to music, I also read books. Books lead me to different imaginations and I feel like I'm in a different world. Also the sea, one of the top things that relaxes me infinitely. If I could, I would spend my whole life on an island.
